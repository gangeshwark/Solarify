Pre-requirements.

Roof


  • Approx. 100 sqft is required for every 1kWp system
  • A shadow free area is necessary, otherwise it will affect generation
  • A flat or sloped roof is suitable

Mounting


  • Panels facing south direction gives best results
  • If installed in any other direction, generation reduces by 3-5%

Power


  • An inverter usually keeps track of daily energy generation
  • Panels should be installed at a maximum of 20m wire length from the inverter
